About Us

Best Cars Dealership is a modern web platform that helps customers discover trusted car dealers and share their buying experiences. Our application provides transparent dealer information and customer reviews to help users make confident car purchasing decisions.

Using advanced full-stack technologies including Django, REST APIs, and cloud deployment, our system allows users to explore dealership details, submit reviews, and analyze customer sentiment. The goal of this project is to create a reliable environment where car buyers can evaluate dealerships based on real customer feedback.
